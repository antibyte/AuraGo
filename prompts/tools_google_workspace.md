### Google Workspace
| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `google_workspace` | Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Docs & Sheets — native integration |

#### Operations
- **Gmail:** `gmail_list`, `gmail_read`, `gmail_send`, `gmail_modify_labels`
- **Calendar:** `calendar_list`, `calendar_create`, `calendar_update`
- **Drive:** `drive_search`, `drive_get_content`
- **Docs:** `docs_get`, `docs_create`, `docs_update`
- **Sheets:** `sheets_get`, `sheets_update`, `sheets_create`

Refer to the google_workspace tool manual for parameter details.
